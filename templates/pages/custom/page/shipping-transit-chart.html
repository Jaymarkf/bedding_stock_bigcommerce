We want your shopping experience to be enjoyable from beginning to end. When you order from us, you can sleep easy (no pun intended!) knowing your bedding products will be delivered to you on time. We ship most orders the same day and all orders within one business day. Tracking information will be made available to you as soon as we ship your item

Our products are manufactures with shipping in mind. All boxes are packed with extra layers of protection to ensure your product makes it to you in flawless condition. In the rare event you do receive a product with a blemish or defect, we will replace that item, no questions asked
We roll and compress our Bedding Stock mattresses so they can fit into a box. This means we can ship your mattress via a common carrier like FedEx as opposed to shipping via a freight carrier. With FedEx, you don't even have to be home to accpet delivery. Freight carriers need you to be home and will give only morning or afternoon deliver windows.

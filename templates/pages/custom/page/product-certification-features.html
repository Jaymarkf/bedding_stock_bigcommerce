Certifications and Features

Bedding Stock offers products that are packed with certifications and features that make sleep more comfortable. Major global independent textile testing and research organizations back our product's quality and safety.

CertiPUR-US ®
Restored Sleep has met the strict standards set by the Alliance for Flexible Polyurethane Foam on the content, emissions and durability of foams. The seal means that our products are safe from environment, health, and fire hazards associated with foams.
Oeko-Text® Standard 100
All Restored Sleep products complies with Oeko- Tex Standard 100 Certification. The seal ensures that the fabrics and materials we use have no harmful substances. Oeko- Text is an internationally renowned textile testing and research body based in Europe.
Feature #01
Activated Charcoal

The bedding industry has been using activated charcoal for many years to absorb the dangerous and harmful odors and help eliminate mold and mildew. Bedding Stock uses activated charcoal from coconut husks.

Feature #02
Breathable White Quilted Poly
Jacquard Fabric

The breathable white poly jacquard fabric makes our
mattress, pillows, and other sleep products always look clean and fresh.

Feature #03
Improved Blood Circulation

We used foams designed to ease the body's pressure
points,eliminate discomfort, and promote healthy blood circulation.

Feature #04
Open Cell Memory Foam

Our open cell foams spread air pressure all over the foam,
allowing them to return to their previous shape after
pressure is applied to it. it also gives way for more airflow
through the material keeping the foam cool and fresh.

Feature #05
Reduces Motion Transfer

Our memory Foam mattresses lesen bounce and absorb
movement. This feature avoids disturbing your sleeping
partner.
